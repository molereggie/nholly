What We Do

Floor Coating Systems for Every Need

From single-car garages to 50,000 sq ft warehouses — we engineer the right coating system for your specific surface, environment, and use.

Garage Floor Coatings

Full-chip flake and solid-color epoxy systems for residential garages. We diamond-grind the surface, repair cracks, then apply a 3-coat system that withstands hot tires, oil, and chemical spills.

Metallic Epoxy Systems

Stunning 3D metallic floors using pigments and pearl additives that create a one-of-a-kind, flowing marble or molten effect. No two floors are exactly alike — a true showpiece surface.

Commercial Floor Coatings

High-build epoxy and MMA systems for warehouses, retail stores, auto shops, and industrial facilities. Chemical-resistant, slip-rated, and compliant with Cal/OSHA safety markings.

Polyurea Top Coats

We finish every system with a 100% solids polyurea or polyaspartic topcoat — 4× harder than standard epoxy. UV-stable, abrasion-resistant, and walk-on ready in hours not days.

Concrete Surface Prep

Professional diamond grinding, shot blasting, and crack repair before any coating goes down. Proper prep is 80% of the job — it's why our floors bond permanently where others peel within a year.

Decorative Flake Systems

Full-broadcast or partial vinyl chip flake systems for a granite-like finish. Completely hides minor surface imperfections, comes in dozens of color blends, and adds texture for slip resistance.

How It Works

Our 5-Step Coating System

Every floor we coat goes through the same disciplined process — the same process we'd use on our own floors.

1

Free Assessment

We inspect your slab, test for moisture, map cracks and spalls, and tell you exactly what needs to be done — no surprises.

2

Diamond Grinding

We mechanically profile the concrete with industrial diamond tooling. This is what separates a 20-year floor from a 2-year floor.

3

Crack & Joint Repair

All cracks, spalls, and control joints are filled and feathered smooth with semi-rigid epoxy filler before any coating is applied.

4

Base Coat Application

We roll 100% solids epoxy base coat, broadcast decorative flake or metallic pigment, then let it cure fully — no shortcuts on timing.

5

Polyurea Topcoat

A final clear polyurea or polyaspartic seal locks everything in. UV-stable, scratch-resistant, and ready for traffic within hours.
